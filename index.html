Welcome — I’m Julia

Julia Lober

Cryospheric Scientist • Scientific Programmer • Open Science Advocate

I’m a Geophysics MSc student at Boise State University, where I study snow and ice to understand our rapidly changing cryosphere. My work sits at the intersection of field science, remote sensing, and open data—I believe that transparent, reproducible science is the foundation of addressing climate change.
